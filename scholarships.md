# Section 16. Institutional Scholarship, Financial Assistance, Grants, and Incentives

## 16.1. Purpose

The University aims to provide financial support to students facing immediate economic and physical challenges and to ensure that students can continue their education despite financial constraints.

## 16.2. Holistic Development

The University recognizes and encourages active student participation in various University activities, promoting holistic development.

## 16.3. Beneficiaries

This program primarily benefits the following groups of students based on the specified qualifications:

- **a.** Students who demonstrate academic commitment and excellence;
- **b.** Underprivileged students; and
- **c.** Indigent students.

## 16.4. General Qualification Requirements

To be eligible for the internally funded **MinSU Scholarship and Financial Assistantship Program**, the applicant must:

- **a.** Be a bona fide student of MinSU;
- **b.** Have a regular load per semester as certified by the University Registrar;
- **c.** Have good moral character as certified by the Guidance Counselor;
- **d.** Have passed all subjects;
- **e.** Submit Certification of Grades duly signed by the University Registrar;
- **f.** Not have any existing scholarship from private or public agencies;
- **g.** Pass the interview conducted by the Screening Committee.

## 16.5. MinSU Scholarship and Financial Assistantship Program

Qualified students shall receive a monthly **stipend/salary/incentive** from the **Special Trust Fund/Student Development Fund**.

### a. Academic Scholars

- **i.** Institutional scholarship is granted to undergraduate students with a computed GWA of:
    - **1.000 – 1.450** (Full Scholarship / President’s Lister)
    - **1.460 – 1.750** (Partial Scholarship / Dean’s Lister)

#### Requirements:

1. No grade below **1.75** in any course;
2. No marks of “Dropped” (official/unofficial), “Deferred,” or “Failed” in any subject including NSTP;
3. Not receiving any other scholarship grants;
4. Enrolled as a regular student with at least **18 units** or the prescribed regular load.

- **ii.** Scholarship is valid for **one semester** and renewable upon meeting the GWA requirement and subject to **availability of funds**.
- **iii.** Monthly stipend:
    - **₱500.00** for Full Scholars
    - **₱300.00** for Partial Scholars
- **iv.** The **Office of the Registrar** shall certify the list of qualified students.

### b. Student Assistantship Program

- **i.** Students with available time and skills may work in university offices and earn student-rate wages to augment allowances.

#### Application Requirements:

- Submit a **letter of intent** to the Office of Student Affairs and Services;
- Attach **parent’s consent**;
- No **failing or incomplete grades** in the previous semester;
- Pass the **pre-hiring screening** by OSAS.

- **ii.** Eligibility Requirements:
    1. Bona fide MinSU student;
    2. Enrolled in not more than **21 units** per semester (certified by Registrar);
    3. Passed all subjects in the previous semester;
    4. Submit Certification of Grades (signed by Registrar);
    5. Pass screening conducted by the **Coordinator/Director of Student Affairs and Services**.

### c. Member of MinSU Accredited Performing Arts Group

#### Full Scholar

1. Active member for at least **one year**;
2. Participation in major **local, regional, or national** performances;
3. **Recommended** by coach/adviser.

#### Partial Scholar

1. Member for at least **one semester**;
2. Attended training and performed in at least **two major University activities**;
3. **Recommended** by coach/adviser.

### d. Economically Deprived / Marginalized Student

- Must have a **General Weighted Average of 2.25**
- Must present a **Certification of Indigency** from the **Municipal Social Welfare and Development Office (MSWDO)**
